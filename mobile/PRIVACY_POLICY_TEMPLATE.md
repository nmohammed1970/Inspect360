### 1. Introduction

Inspect360 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you use our App:

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, phone number, and other profile information you provide during registration or profile updates
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials (email and password) - stored securely and encrypted
- **User Profile**: First name, last name, profile photo, skills, qualifications, and other professional information

#### 2.2 Inspection Data
- **Property Inspections**: Inspection reports, photos, notes, conditions, and digital signatures
- **Maintenance Requests**: Maintenance issue descriptions, photos, priority levels, and status updates
- **Asset Inventory**: Property assets, inventory items, and related information

#### 2.3 Device Information
- **Device Type**: iOS or Android device information
- **App Usage**: Features used, timestamps, and interaction data
- **Technical Data**: Device identifiers, operating system version, app version

#### 2.4 Location Data
- **Property Locations**: Addresses and location data for properties you inspect (only when you provide this information)

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Authentication**: To verify your identity and provide secure access to the App
- **App Functionality**: To enable core features including inspections, maintenance management, and asset inventory
- **Data Synchronization**: To sync your data across devices and maintain offline functionality
- **Communication**: To send you notifications, updates, and important information about your account
- **Service Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
- **Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

### 4. How We Store Your Information

#### 4.1 Local Storage
- **Secure Storage**: Your authentication credentials and user session data are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (iOS Keychain/Android Keystore)
- **Offline Data**: Inspection data and other app data are stored locally in an encrypted database for offline access
- **Data Retention**: Local data is retained until you log out or delete the App

#### 4.2 Server Storage
- **Cloud Storage**: Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption in transit and at rest
- **Data Retention**: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
- **Backup**: Regular backups are performed to ensure data availability and recovery

### 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Secure Storage**: Sensitive data is stored using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain/Android Keystore)
- **Access Controls**: Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- **Session Management**: Secure session cookies are used for authentication
- **Regular Updates**: We regularly update our security practices and infrastructure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With third-party service providers who assist in operating our App (hosting, analytics, etc.) - these providers are bound by confidentiality agreements
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information
